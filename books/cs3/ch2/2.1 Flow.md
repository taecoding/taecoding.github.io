# Logical Operator
connect and modify boolean expressions
- NOT: !
- OR: ||
- AND: &&

# Comparison Operators
compare two primitive values
- EQUAL                 ==
- NOT EQUAL             !=
- GREATER THAN          >
- LESS THAN             <
- LESS THAN OR EQUAL    <=
